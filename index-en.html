How it works

Use the app to set your location and whether the plants are outdoors (rain exposure) or protected. The system skips watering on rainy days to save water.

During the adaptation period, the app asks if pots are too dry or too wet and suggests fine‑tuning — pot by pot — for perfect balance.

Aguatudo also scales to larger gardens and vegetable beds: just connect it to higher‑capacity equipment.
