How it works

If your plants are in outdoor areas exposed to rain, you can skip irrigation on rainy days — saving water.

During the adaptation period, you simply observe whether pots are dry or overwatered and fine-tune each outlet using the flow-adjustment accessory — achieving perfect balance.

Aguatudo also supports larger gardens and urban farms: just connect it to higher-scale irrigation equipment.
