Welcome to Aguatudo

Aguatudo is a smart irrigation system designed to keep your plants alive and thriving. It connects to the internet to adjust watering time according to weather and humidity conditions.

It comes with a DIY-style kit, ideal for apartments: spike drippers, transparent and flexible tubing that makes adjustment easy in indoor environments.

With the app, you can enter your location and the type of environment, such as outdoor, indoor, or balcony. This helps the system avoid unnecessary watering on rainy days, saving water. During the adaptation period, the app asks whether the pots are dry or soaked, helping to adjust watering times.

Aguatudo can also be used in large gardens and vegetable plots by connecting it to larger-scale equipment. For more information, contact us.
